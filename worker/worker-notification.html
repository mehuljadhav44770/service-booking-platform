Worker Guidelines & Job Rules
Job Acceptance Rules
  • Accept jobs only when you are available and nearby.
  • Once accepted, timely service completion is expected.
  • Repeated no-shows may affect your rating.
Job Rejection Policy
  • You may reject jobs for valid reasons only.
  • Valid reasons include:
    • 📍 Job location too far
    • ⏰ Slot unavailable
    • 🚨 Emergency
    • 🛠 Wrong service category
  • Rejecting jobs without valid reason repeatedly is discouraged.
⚠️ Penalty Rule:
If you reject more than 3 jobs without valid reasons:
  • Your account will be temporarily restricted.
  • You will not receive new job requests for a short duration.
  • Restrictions are automatically lifted after cooldown.
These rules help ensure fair job distribution and better customer experience. Continuous misuse may impact your worker rating.
